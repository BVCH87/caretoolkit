Contact

We read every message. Whether you've spotted an error, want to suggest a tool, or have a question about something on the site, please get in touch.

Email

The best way to reach us is by email:

hello@caretoolkit.co.uk

We aim to reply within three working days. Please note we cannot give personalised financial, legal, or medical advice — for that, please contact a regulated professional or a free advice service such as Age UK Advice (0800 678 1602).

What we can help with

  • Spotting inaccuracies in our tools or content
  • Suggestions for new tools you'd find useful
  • Questions about how a tool works
  • Press, partnership, or general enquiries

What we can't help with

  • Specific advice on your parent's situation — we're not qualified to give it
  • Recommending individual care homes
  • Financial planning or legal questions specific to your circumstances

For those, please contact Age UK, Alzheimer's Society, Carers UK, or a regulated solicitor or financial adviser specialising in later-life planning.

Care Toolkit provides general information, not financial or legal advice. Always seek professional guidance before making decisions about a parent's care or finances.
