Why Care Toolkit exists

The UK care system is complicated, fragmented across four nations, and changes most years. The information you need is scattered across gov.uk, charity websites, council pages, and forums. Most people only encounter it when they're already in crisis — a parent in hospital, a sudden diagnosis, a phone call from a worried sibling.

Care Toolkit exists to give you straightforward, reliable starting points. Every tool is free, every figure is sourced from official guidance, and we'll never ask you to sign up or hand over personal data. We're not a care provider, financial adviser, or legal firm — we're an independent resource trying to make a hard moment a little less overwhelming.
